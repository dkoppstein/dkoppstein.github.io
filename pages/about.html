About Me

Photo shot by Gong, at Dishui Lake, Shanghai, 2014

I am now a Ph.D. candidate with Prof. Yaohui Jin at Shanghai Jiao Tong Univ.. I received my B.S. (2010) of Optical Information and Science Technology at Xidian University, Xi'an, China.

My research interests come from the measurement and analysis of network traffic, especially the renewed models and characteristics of networks traffic, with the data mining techniques and high performance processing platforms like Network Processors and distributed processing systems like Hadoop/MapReduce or Spark.

If you are interested in my articles, researches, or projects, you can reach me via email or other partially instant messages like github.

Enjoy! :-)
